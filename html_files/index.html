Welcome to Huescape

Discover Huescape!

Huescape is a vibrant puzzle game designed to challenge your mind while immersing you in a world of colors. With no storyline to distract from the core mechanics, it's all about solving intricate, engaging puzzles built around the dynamic interplay of color-based elements.

Whether you're a casual player seeking a relaxing challenge or a puzzle enthusiast craving complexity, Huescape delivers an experience packed with thought-provoking gameplay and endless possibilities.

About Huescape

Game Features

  • Color-Based Mechanics: Harness the power of Red (Fire), Green (Nature), and Blue (Water) to solve puzzles in creative ways.
  • Diverse Levels: Each stage introduces fresh mechanics, keeping the experience exciting and challenging.
  • Deep Puzzle Design: A mix of intuitive and complex puzzles ensures players of all skill levels can enjoy the journey.
  • Expanding Features: With unique abilities linked to colors, you’ll discover new possibilities with each level.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main objective of Huescape?

Huescape is a puzzle game where colors dictate gameplay mechanics. The goal is to solve intricate puzzles using color-based logic.

Are there any hints or tips available for solving puzzles?

Unfortunately, there won't be any hints provided throughout the game. However, the puzzles work up in difficulty so you just need to push yourself a little bit extra!

Are there any time limits or penalties in the game?

Nope! You can take as long as you want.

Are there any achievements or rewards for completing levels?

You get to tell all your friends that you're better than them because you beat Huescape and they didn't.

What did you use to develop Huescape

Huescape was developed with the Godot 4.4 Engine. All the pizel art was created in Aseprite.
