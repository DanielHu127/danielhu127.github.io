Huescape - A Retro Puzzle Adventure

Game Concept

Huescape is a puzzle game where colors dictate mechanics. Players must use Red (Fire), Green (Nature), and Blue (Water) strategically to complete levels.

Target Audience

Designed for puzzle lovers, casual gamers, and retro enthusiasts.
